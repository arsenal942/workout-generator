About

Unique and Challenging workouts.

Workout Generator is an easy to use, open source web application that requires no database to run. Workout Generator is designed for generating workout structures and programs that are unique and challenging. It utilises a variety of training methods and exercises to generate a wide range of workouts. Every workout is unique, different and will challenge you. You can tailor the generated workout to your conditions and apply various filtering options to refine the results.

You can follow the development progress on the official GitHub Repository.
